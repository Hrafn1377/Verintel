About Verintel

Why we built this, and what we're trying to fix.

The Problem

The job market is broken. Fake postings, ghost listings, and predatory recruiters have turned job searching into a minefield. Job seekers spend hours crafting applications for roles that don't exist, interviewing for positions that were never going to be filled, and dealing with recruiters who vanish the moment they're no longer useful.

Traditional job boards have no incentive to fix this. They make money from employers posting listings — real or not. The job seeker pays the price.

What Verintel Does

Verintel verifies every employer and every job listing before it reaches job seekers. Each listing is assigned a trust score based on our verification process — Verified, Warning, or Flagged — so you know exactly what you're looking at before you apply.

No more guessing. No more wasted applications. No more being ghosted by a job that was never real.

Who It's For

Verintel is for job seekers who are tired of being burned — developers, designers, writers, marketers, and anyone else navigating a hiring process that wasn't built in their favor.

It's also for employers who want to stand out. A verified listing on Verintel signals to candidates that you're a legitimate employer worth their time.

Our Commitment

Verintel is free for job seekers. Always. We believe access to a safe, verified job search should not come at a cost to the people who need it most.
