Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Verintel.

About Verintel

What is Verintel?

Verintel is a trust-scored job platform built to fix a broken hiring process. Every job listing and every employer is verified before it reaches job seekers — so when you apply, you know it's real.

How is Verintel different from Indeed or LinkedIn?

Unlike traditional job boards, Verintel verifies every employer and job listing before it goes live. Each listing receives a trust score so you can see at a glance how legitimate it is. We exist specifically to eliminate fake postings, ghost jobs, and predatory recruiters.

Who is Verintel for?

Verintel is for job seekers who are tired of wasting time on fake or misleading job postings, and for employers who want to stand out as verified and legitimate.

Job Seekers

Is Verintel free for job seekers?

Yes. Verintel is completely free for job seekers, always.

How does verification work?

When an employer submits a job listing, Verintel runs it through a verification process that checks the legitimacy of the employer and the listing itself. Listings that pass receive a Verified status. Listings that raise concerns are flagged or given a warning before they ever reach job seekers.

What is a trust score?

A trust score is a rating assigned to each job listing based on our verification process. It tells you at a glance how legitimate a listing is before you spend time applying.

How do I know a job posting is real?

Every listing on Verintel displays its verification status — Verified, Warning, or Flagged. We recommend only applying to Verified listings. If something still feels off, use the report button on the listing.

How do I report a suspicious listing?

Click the report button on any job listing and provide details about your concern. Our team reviews every report.

Employers

How do I post a job?

Create an employer account, complete your profile, and submit your job listing. Your first posting is free. All listings go through verification before going live.

How does employer verification work?

When you create an employer account, Verintel verifies your business details to confirm you are a legitimate employer. This protects job seekers and gives your listings more credibility.

How much does it cost to post a job?

Your first job posting is free. After that you have three options:

  • Pay per post — $20 per listing
  • Monthly — $49/month for unlimited postings
  • Annual — $399/year for unlimited postings

How long does verification take?

Verification is typically completed quickly. In some cases it may take up to 24 hours. If your listing has not gone live after 24 hours, contact hrafn@joinverintel.com.

Privacy & Data

What data does Verintel collect?

Verintel collects the information you provide when creating an account and using the platform. We do not sell your data to third parties. See our privacy policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at hrafn@joinverintel.com and we will process your account deletion request.
