Help Center

Common issues and how to resolve them.

Account

Can't log in

Double check your email and password are correct. If you registered with Google or GitHub, use that login method instead. If the issue persists, use the forgot password link on the login page.

Didn't receive verification email

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, wait a few minutes and try again. Still nothing? Contact us at hrafn@joinverintel.com and we'll sort it out.

How to reset your password

Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How to update your account details

Go to your profile settings. From there you can update your name, email, and other personal information.

Job Seekers

Can't apply to a listing

Make sure you are logged in. Some listings may require a completed profile before applying. Check that your profile is fully filled out and try again.

Listing appears inactive or expired

Job listings expire when the employer closes the role or the posting period ends. If you believe a listing should still be active, use the report button on the listing page.

How to report a suspicious listing

Click the report button on any job listing. Provide as much detail as possible about why the listing seems suspicious. Our team reviews all reports and will act accordingly.

Employers

Job posting not showing up

New listings go through a verification check before appearing on the platform. This typically takes a short time. If your listing has not appeared after 24 hours, contact support.

Verification taking longer than expected

Verification is usually quick but can take up to 24 hours in some cases. If it has been longer than that, contact us at hrafn@joinverintel.com with your posting details.

How to edit or remove a posting

Go to your employer dashboard and select the listing you want to edit or remove. Changes to active listings may require re-verification.

Billing and payment issues

For any billing questions or payment issues, contact us at hrafn@joinverintel.com with your account details and a description of the issue.

General

How to contact support

Email us at hrafn@joinverintel.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

How to report a bug

Email hrafn@joinverintel.com with a description of the bug, what you were doing when it happened, and the device and browser you were using. Screenshots are helpful if you can include them.
